    Seoul Gallery BI

    Seoul Spiral of Inspiration

    The Seoul Gallery BI utilizes the initial ‘S’,
    symbolizing the identity
    and symbolism of the space,
    to express an ascending and circulating
    ‘spiral of inspiration.’

    The interplay of straight and curved lines
    visually conveys a dynamic sense
    of movement and form,
    representing the growth and
    harmony created
    through the collaboration
    between the Seoul Metropolitan
    Government and its citizens.

    By connecting with the city brand
    “SEOUL MY SOUL”
    and its vibrant color palette,
    the design reflects the lively
    and multifaceted character of Seoul.
